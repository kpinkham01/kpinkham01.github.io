About Me

My name is Kayleen Pinkham and I am a college student at Saint Marys College, Notre Dame, Indiana. My major is in English Writing and I plan to pursue a minor in film and English Literature. I did not go to college for computer coding and I had assumed that it was something I would never be able to pursue with any seriousness. My oppertunity to rectify that belief and gain the coding experience I desired through a suprise winter term coding class. Due to Covid-19 changing the structure and timing of the semester left a giant whole over the winterbreak, thus allowing students such as myself the time necessary to take a class like this. Before this class I had no experince coding beyond a single class over a year prior that dealt exclusivly with python, something that was not necissary for the first half of class. Thus, in November of 2020 I began my coding journey focusing in on JavaScript. Those three weeks of training and practice have now culminated in this portfolio and associated projects. As of December 2020 I have not yet completed my training in computer coding, with two weeks of python in 2021 to look forward too, this will be updated to accomadate the new coding projects and associated experiences.
